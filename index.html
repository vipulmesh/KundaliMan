About KundaliMan

KundaliMan is a Vedic numerology tool that decodes your birth date into a rich personal blueprint — covering personality, career, love, finance, spirituality, and shareable aura cards. It combines ancient Indian numerology (Mulank & Bhagyank) with a clean, modern interface.

About the Creator

Hi! I'm Vipul Meshram, a developer and creative technologist passionate about blending ancient wisdom with modern tech. KundaliMan started as a personal curiosity project about numerology and grew into a full-featured app.
